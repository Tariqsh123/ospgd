Building a Strong Pakistan–Germany Connection

One Station Pak–German Desk (OSPGD) is a dedicated platform designed to strengthen economic, educational, and technological relations between Pakistan and Germany. We aim to create meaningful bridges that connect businesses, institutions, students, and professionals under one unified system of opportunity and growth.

Through structured collaboration, skill development programs, and international networking, we help individuals and organizations access global markets, modern training standards, and cross-border partnerships that lead to long-term success.

Our mission is to simplify international cooperation by providing a single, reliable hub where ideas turn into action, and connections turn into real opportunities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about One Station Pak–German Desk (OSPGD)

What is OSPGD?
One Station Pak–German Desk (OSPGD) is a 360° economic facilitation platform connecting Pakistan and Germany in trade, skills, innovation, and investment.
Who can join OSPGD?
Businesses, students, professionals, investors, and institutions interested in Pakistan–Germany collaboration can join OSPGD.
Is OSPGD a government organization?
No, OSPGD is an independent facilitation platform focused on economic and trade bridging between Pakistan and Germany.
How does OSPGD help in trade?
We provide B2B matchmaking, market entry support, import/export facilitation, and strategic partnerships.
How can I contact OSPGD?
